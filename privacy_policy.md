# Privacy Policy for YouTube Liberated

**Last Updated:** July 4, 2026

Yuvatech Solutions USA LLC ("we", "us", or "our") operates the **YouTube Liberated** browser extension. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our data collection and handling practices.

## 1. No Collection of Personal Information
The **YouTube Liberated** extension operates entirely client-side. 
- We do **not** collect, store, process, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
- We do **not** collect, track, or record your browsing history, IP address, device information, search queries, or video viewing behavior.
- There are no telemetry systems, analytics trackers, or remote logging services bundled inside the extension.

## 2. Permissions and How They Are Used
To provide its core decluttering functionality, the extension requires the following browser permissions:
- **`storage`**: Used solely to store your layout preferences (e.g., whether the Ads, Shorts, or Playables filters are turned ON or OFF). This configuration is saved locally on your device using the browser's storage API and is never sent over the network.
- **Host Permission (`*://*.youtube.com/*`)**: Used to inject local stylesheets and javascript files to hide unwanted layout elements (such as ads, Shorts shelves, and Playables games) and bypass page visibility events on YouTube. The extension does not access or modify data on any other websites.

## 3. Third-Party Sharing
Since we do not collect any user data, we do not share, sell, trade, rent, or distribute any user information to third-party entities, advertising networks, or data brokers.

## 4. Compliance with Store Policies
This policy and the extension comply with the Chrome Web Store Developer Agreement and Google's User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
