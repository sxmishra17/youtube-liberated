Here are the complete assets for uploading and listing **YouTube Liberated** on the Firefox Add-ons (AMO) portal.

## 📝 Store Descriptions

### 1. Short Description (Max 150 characters)
> Lightweight YouTube extension to block ads, hide Shorts/Community posts, and keep playing audio in the background on Firefox for Android & Desktop.

### 2. Full Description (Rich Text / Markdown)
```markdown
YouTube Liberated is a lightweight, high-performance extension designed to return control of the YouTube experience back to you. Free up your interface, block distractions, and unlock premium features—completely free.

Optimized for both Firefox for Desktop and Firefox for Android, it functions seamlessly on mobile devices to give you a true YouTube Premium-style music and video experience.

🌟 Key Features:
• 🚫 Ad-Free Playback (Zero-Flicker Blocker)
  Blocks all video ads (pre-rolls, mid-rolls), homepage banners, feed ads, search ads, and player overlay banners. The custom engine skips ads instantly and mutes their audio so you never notice them.
• 🎵 Background Audio Playback (Premium Music Mode)
  Keep listening to your favorite music, podcasts, and videos when Firefox is minimized or the screen is locked on Android and Desktop. (No more forced pauses).
• 📱 Shorts Redirection & Blocker
  Hides the vertical Shorts shelf grids from your homepage and subscriptions feed to keep your feed clean. Additionally, any attempt to visit a /shorts/ URL automatically redirects it to play in the standard YouTube watch player.
• 📰 Community Posts Blocker
  Cleans up subscription feeds and the home feed by completely removing Community Posts and dynamic text updates.
• 🎛️ Independent Toggle Controls
  Tailor your experience. Choose exactly what you want to block or allow with simple, instant-apply toggles from the toolbar popup menu (no page reload required).

🔒 Privacy & Data Collection:
We believe in absolute privacy. YouTube Liberated does not track, collect, store, or transmit any user data, search histories, or personal identification. All preferences are stored locally in your browser’s secure storage.

Developed By
Yuvatech Solutions USA LLC
```
